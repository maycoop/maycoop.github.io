Amenities

Hot and Cold Water included. You must pay your own electrical/heat and cable.
All units have a storage locker in the basement.

Other facilities at Mayflower CO-OP include:

  • Large meeting room with full kitchen and 2 washrooms (handicapped accessible).
  • Large lounge with table & chairs, couches, puzzle table, TV, library of books and DVD movies
  • Laundry room with 5 washers ($1.00 per load) and 8 dryers (25 cents for every 12 minutes) open 7 days a week from 8:00 am to 9:00 pm.
  • Outdoor community barbeque

Participation is Mandatory!

Every member is required to participate a minimum of 24 hrs per fiscal year. Participation includes sitting on a committee such as Social, Membership, Building Maintenance, Security, Newsletter, etc., or on the Board of Directors.

Interested in living at Mayflower CO-OP?

Becoming a member

An application form must be filled out and submitted to the co-op office. Suitable candidates are invited to attend an interview conducted by the Membership Committee.

Acceptance

Verify landlord references, employment and credit history Proof of income (up-to-date information must be provided). Note: submitting an application are placed in the waiting list file; this does not guarantee acceptance. Applications are kept on file for 1 year; if you wish to remain on file past 1 year, you must contact the co-op to keep you application active.

Meetings

Members are required to attend a minimum of 2 General Meetings per year.
